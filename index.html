About the Guild

Blue Coast Software is an independent guild of tools and games, built in the spirit of craft rather than commerce. We approach software as a workshop discipline—lean, deliberate, and designed to endure—favoring clarity over flash and substance over scale.

Our projects blend retro-futurist aesthetics with modern engineering, drawing on the heritage of early computing, the imagination of high fantasy, and the rigor of classical design.

We value clear craft and long horizons: tools meant to serve families and communities for decades, games meant to delight while carrying depth, and code written not just to function, but to last.

Blue Coast is where story, engineering, and design converge—our guild hall on the digital shore.
