ABOUT BLUE COAST

Blue Coast Software is a Ventura-based software workshop specializing in terminal UIs, web applications, and custom business tools. We bring coastal discipline to digital craft.

Our philosophy is simple: terminal-sharp software with retro discipline and coastal calm. We build tools that are efficient, elegant, and built to last.

Whether you need a command-line interface, a web application, or a custom business solution, we approach every project with the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.
